# CHAT SHORTCUTS

These natural language prompts are recognized directly in chat without needing slash commands.

## Balance
- What's my balance?
- Whats my balance?
- What is my balance?
- Show my balance
- Tell me my balance

## Debts
- What are my debts?
- Show my debts
- List my debts
- Tell me my debts
- List debts
