# CHAT SHORTCUTS

These natural language prompts are recognized directly in chat without needing slash commands.

## Balance
- What's my balance?
- Whats my balance?
- What is my balance?
- Show my balance
- Tell me my balance

## Debts
- What are my debts?
- Show my debts
- List my debts
- Tell me my debts
- List debts

## Recurring bills
- Show my recurring bills
- List my recurring bills
- Show recurring bills
- List recurring bills
- Show my recurring items
- List my recurring items

- Whats my spending summary?
- What is my spending summary?
- Tell me my spending summary
- Show my summary
- What's my summary?

- Tell me my summary

## Balance on
